For Encryption:
python encryption_win.py "C:\Users\John\Documents" "C:\Users\John\Pictures" "C:\keys\secret.key"

To Restore:
:: Step 1: Extract ZIPs (using 7-Zip, WinRAR, or built-in)
7z x "C:\Users\John\Documents.zip" -o"C:\restore\docs" -p"YourPassword"

:: Step 2: Decrypt
python decryption_win.py "C:\restore\docs" "C:\restore\pics" "C:\keys\secret.key"
